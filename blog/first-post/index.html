Into the Wild: A Week in the Maasai Mara

June 15, 2026

I’ve been going to the Maasai Mara for the better part of a decade, and it never gets old. This time, the goal was simple — photograph a lioness with her cubs at golden hour.

Three days of waiting. Dawn drives, afternoon heat, false alarms. On the fourth morning, she appeared.

The light was soft, filtered through Harmattan dust. She walked right past our jeep, cubs in tow, close enough that I could hear her breath.

I got the shot. But more than that, I got the reminder: patience isn’t waiting — it’s being fully present.

Gear used

  • Sony A7R V + 70-200mm f/2.8
  • Gitzo tripod
  • Beanbag for low-angle vehicle work

Location

Maasai Mara National Reserve, Kenya — June 2026

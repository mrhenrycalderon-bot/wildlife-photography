About Me

I'm a wildlife and travel photographer based in Nairobi, Kenya. My work takes me from the frozen peaks of the Himalayas to the sun-scorched savannahs of East Africa — anywhere the wild still roams free.

I've been shooting for over a decade, driven by a simple belief: you protect what you fall in love with, and you fall in love with what you truly see. Every image here is an invitation to see the natural world a little more clearly.

When I'm not behind a lens, I'm planning the next expedition, editing in the field, or advocating for conservation initiatives across the continent.
